## What is Peec.ai Looker Studio Integration?

Peec.ai's Looker Studio community connector allows you to seamlessly integrate your AI search visibility data into Google's powerful data visualization platform. Transform your keyword performance metrics into insightful dashboards and reports that help you monitor and improve your company's visibility across AI search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini.
